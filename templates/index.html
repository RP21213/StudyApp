Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Foci

What is Foci and how does it work?

Foci is an AI-powered study platform that transforms your course materials into interactive learning tools. Simply upload your notes, PDFs, or paste text, and our AI instantly creates flashcards, quizzes, summaries, and exam predictions.

What content formats are supported?

We support PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoint presentations, plain text, and even live audio from lectures. Our AI can process virtually any study material you have.

How does the AI tutor work?

Our AI tutor analyzes your uploaded content and can answer questions, provide explanations, and give personalized feedback. It adapts to your learning style and helps clarify concepts you're struggling with.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your study materials are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your content with third parties, and you can delete your data at any time.

What's the pricing and cost?

Foci offers a free tier with basic features. Our Pro plan starts at $9.99/month and includes advanced features like exam predictions, unlimited materials, and priority AI processing.

Can I use Foci for group study?

Absolutely! Foci includes study group features where you can collaborate with classmates, share materials, and work together on projects. Perfect for team assignments and group exam prep.
