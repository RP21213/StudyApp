Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free plan?

Yes! Our Basic plan is completely free and allows you to create up to 2 study hubs with basic notes and flashcard tools. It's a great way to get started.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account settings. You will retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

What makes Foci different from Quizlet/Notion?

Foci is an all-in-one AI-powered study platform. While tools like Quizlet focus on flashcards and Notion on notes, Foci integrates everything. It actively transforms your materials into a complete set of study tools, from notes to quizzes, essay drafts, and even predicts exam questions.

Is my data secure?

Your data security is our top priority. All your uploaded documents and generated content are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties.
